Welcome to the Dyslexia Helper App!

This application is designed to provide tools and resources to assist individuals with dyslexia. You can explore various sections like interactive games, educational quizzes, and a pronunciation helper to improve reading and language skills.

Use the "Settings" button on the top right to customize the appearance of the website for a more comfortable reading experience.

Interactive Games

Engage with fun and educational games designed to improve phonics, word recognition, and memory.

Educational Quizzes

Test your knowledge and reinforce learning with quizzes on spelling, vocabulary, and comprehension.

Pronunciation Helper

Practice saying words and get feedback to improve your speech and phonological awareness.
